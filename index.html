Nettie Alevropoulos-Borrill

Researcher in Computational Biological Physics

I am a postgraduate researcher at the University of York in the Noy Group. My research focuses on computational experiments of biomolecules, specifically nanomachines involved in DNA replication. My work is funded by the EPSRC and is supported through the use of local and regional computer clusters including JADE and CSD3.
